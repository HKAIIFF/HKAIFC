Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about HKAIFC membership

What are the requirements for each membership tier?

Individual members need relevant professional experience or education in AI film. Institutional members must be accredited educational or research organizations. Corporate members require ≥HK$10 million registered capital and AI film business involvement. Founding members need significant industry contributions and 5-year commitment.

How long does the application process take?

Individual and institutional applications typically take 5-7 business days. Corporate member applications take 10-15 business days due to additional verification. Founding member applications require 15-20 business days for comprehensive review by our board.

Can I upgrade my membership level later?

Yes, you can upgrade your membership at any time. The price difference will be prorated based on your remaining membership period. Contact our membership team to discuss upgrade options and benefits transition.

What happens if my application is not approved?

If your application is not approved, we'll provide detailed feedback on areas for improvement. You can reapply after addressing the identified issues. For founding and corporate memberships, we offer consultation calls to help strengthen future applications.

Are there any geographic restrictions for membership?

HKAIFC membership is open globally. However, some benefits like local networking events may be geographically specific. We're expanding our regional presence to serve members worldwide with localized support and services.

What is the renewal process for membership?

Membership renewal is automatic unless you notify us otherwise. We'll send renewal notices 60 days before expiration. Members in good standing can renew with simplified documentation. Multi-year discounts are available for advance renewals.
