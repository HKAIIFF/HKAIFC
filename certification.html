5-Dimensional Assessment Framework

Comprehensive evaluation system covering all aspects of AI-native film excellence, from technical innovation to social impact

AI-Native Level (1-5)

Measures the degree of AI integration and autonomy in film creation, from basic AI assistance to fully autonomous AI-driven production.

Level 5 Requirements:
  • AI Dominance Rate: ≥90%
  • Real-time Response: ≤50ms
  • Personalization: ≥95%
  • Ending Generation: ≥10⁶ variations
  • Autonomous Evolution: Full support
Content Quality (1-5)

Evaluates technical excellence, creative value, user experience, cultural impact, and commercial viability of the content.

Level 5 Requirements:
  • Technical Quality: ≥95 points
  • Creative Value: ≥90 points
  • User Experience: ≥90 points
  • Cultural Impact: ≥85 points
  • Commercial Value: ≥80 points
Innovation Technology (1-5)

Assesses breakthrough technologies, patent applications, technical barriers, and market uniqueness.

Level 5 Requirements:
  • Innovation: International leading
  • Patents: ≥10 applications
  • Technical Barriers: Extremely high
  • Market Uniqueness: ≥95%
  • Technology Readiness: TRL 9
Commercial Maturity (1-5)

Evaluates market revenue, user scale, market share, profitability, and expansion capabilities.

Level 5 Requirements:
  • Market Revenue: ≥HK$100 million
  • User Scale: ≥1 million
  • Market Share: ≥20%
  • Profitability: Continuous profit
  • Expansion: Globalization
Social Responsibility (1-5)

Measures ethical compliance, social contribution, environmental protection, data security, and cultural heritage preservation.

Level 5 Requirements:
  • Ethical Compliance: 100%
  • Social Impact: Significant
  • Environment: Carbon neutral
  • Data Security: Zero incidents
  • Cultural Heritage: Active promotion
Future Dimensions

Reserved framework for emerging technologies including Metaverse Integration, Blockchain Application, and Sustainability metrics.
