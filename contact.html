Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about HKAIFC services and processes

How long does the certification process take?

Work certification typically takes 4-6 weeks from application submission to certificate issuance. Professional certification varies from 2-4 weeks depending on the level and complexity.

What are the certification fees?

Work certification is ¬•5,000 RMB for all levels. Professional certification ranges from ¬•3,000 RMB (Levels 1-3) to ¬•30,000 RMB (Levels 4-5). Members receive significant discounts.

Do you offer consultation services?

Yes, we provide expert consultation services at ¬•1,000/hour. We also offer standards interpretation training, certification guidance, and customized corporate training programs.

How can I become a member?

We offer various membership levels: Individual (¬•5,000/year), Institutional (¬•50,000/year), Corporate (¬•100,000/year), and Founding Members (¬•500,000/year). Each level has specific requirements and benefits.
