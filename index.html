Founding Organizations

HKAIFC was established through the collaborative vision of Hong Kong's leading AI film industry organizations, uniting expertise in festivals, markets, education, distribution, and investment to create the world's most comprehensive standards authority.

Hong Kong AI International Film Festival Association

Premier organization for AI film festivals, competitions, and industry events, establishing global recognition standards for AI-native filmmaking excellence and innovation.

Hong Kong AI Film Market

The world's leading marketplace for AI film trading, distribution, and commercial licensing, connecting creators with global audiences and establishing market value benchmarks.

Hong Kong AI Film Academy

Advanced educational institution providing comprehensive training in AI filmmaking, technical skills development, and professional certification for industry practitioners.

Hong Kong AI International Film Festival Submission Center

Global submission platform facilitating AI film festival participation worldwide, standardizing submission processes and quality evaluation criteria across international venues.

Hong Kong AI Film Foundation

Strategic investment and funding organization supporting AI film innovation, providing capital, resources, and business development expertise for emerging projects.

Industry News & Updates

Stay informed about the latest developments in AI film standards, certification milestones, and industry transformation initiatives from HKAIFC and our global partners

January 20, 2025

HKAIFC Officially Launches Global AI Film Standards Framework

The Hong Kong AI Film Standards Committee officially launches its comprehensive certification ecosystem with over 50 industry standards, establishing the world's first authoritative framework for AI-native film production, quality assessment, and professional development across the global entertainment industry.

January 15, 2025

Strategic Partnership with ISO Establishes Global Recognition

HKAIFC signs landmark cooperation agreement with the International Organization for Standardization (ISO) for mutual recognition of AI film standards, creating the foundation for worldwide acceptance and harmonization of industry certification and quality assurance protocols.

January 12, 2025

First AI Films Achieve Prestigious Level 5 Certification

Three groundbreaking AI-native films become the first to achieve HKAIFC's highest Level 5 certification, demonstrating excellence across all five assessment dimensions and setting new industry benchmarks for technical innovation, creative quality, and commercial viability.

January 8, 2025

Global Membership Program Opens to Industry Leaders

HKAIFC launches its exclusive membership program for organizations worldwide, offering founding member benefits including standards development participation, priority certification services, and access to the global AI film industry leadership network and governance opportunities.

January 5, 2025

Professional Certification Program Launches with 5-Level System

Comprehensive professional development program launches featuring 5-level certification system across multiple specialization tracks, providing AI film industry professionals with internationally recognized credentials and career advancement pathways from junior level to ambassador status.

January 2, 2025

Multi-Language Platform Supports 40+ Global Languages

HKAIFC certification platform now supports over 40 languages with AI-powered real-time translation, making standards, certification services, and professional development programs accessible to global participants in their native languages, fostering worldwide industry adoption.
