Mission & Vision

Leading the transformation of the AI film industry through authoritative standards and certification

Our Mission

To become the authoritative institution for AI-native film industry standards development, certification services, and technological innovation in the Asia-Pacific region, promoting standardized development of AI film technology and fostering a healthy and prosperous industry ecosystem.

Our Vision

By 2030, to establish a globally comprehensive AI film standards system, become an industry-recognized authoritative certification body, and help the AI-native film industry create market value exceeding $100 billion through innovative standards and professional certification services.

Development Roadmap

Strategic three-year plan to establish global leadership in AI film standards

2025

Foundation Building Year

Establishing organizational infrastructure, developing core standards, and launching certification services.

  • Complete committee organizational structure
  • Release first batch of 10 core technical standards
  • Develop 10 founding members and 50 corporate members
  • Complete 100 work certifications and 500 professional certifications
  • Establish Hong Kong headquarters office
2026

Expansion & Development Year

Scaling operations, enhancing technology platforms, and expanding international presence.

  • Release complete standards system (50 standards)
  • Develop 200 corporate and 100 institutional members
  • Complete 500 work certifications and 2,000 professional certifications
  • Establish 3 overseas cooperation centers
  • Launch AI-assisted assessment system
2027

Internationalization Year

Achieving global recognition and establishing comprehensive international cooperation network.

  • Establish branches in 5 countries/regions
  • Sign cooperation agreements with 10 international organizations
  • Develop 1,000 member institutions globally
  • Complete 2,000 work certifications and 10,000 professional certifications
  • Launch next-generation AI-Native 3.0 standards
